Privacy Policy

What The Spire collects, how it is used, and what stays private. For how members are expected to behave, see the Rules.

What we collect

  • Account basics: email address, username, password (stored only as a hash nobody can view), and date of birth, plus any optional profile details you add. Your date of birth is used to classify your account as adult or minor for 18+ age gating; it is never shown to other members.
  • Technical records: IP addresses, browser type, and sign-in timestamps, kept while your account exists and used for security, ban enforcement, and detecting duplicate accounts. Only staff with specific permissions can view them.
  • Your content: what you post and upload, retained per the site's message retention settings and any per-room expiry.

What staff can and cannot see

  • Administrators cannot read private rooms, whispers, or direct messages; the system does not expose them. They see only surrounding details such as room names, presence, and timestamps. Staff can act on private content only when a participant reports it with evidence (reporting a direct message attaches a copy).
  • Access to emails, dates of birth, and IP records is gated behind specific staff permissions. Moderation records, reports, and evidence are retained to document decisions and prevent ban evasion.

Cookies and your device

  • No advertising or tracking cookies, and no third-party tracker scripts. The one cookie we set is a short-lived security cookie during optional Google sign-in, deleted after use.
  • Your sign-in session is a token in your browser's storage, sent only to The Spire. Small conveniences (panel sizes, drafts, dismissed notices, image-hiding choices) stay on your device; preferences that follow you (theme, language, reading font) are saved to your account.

Analytics

  • Traffic is measured with our own built-in, cookieless analytics; no outside analytics company receives anything. Records hold pages used, referring site, and approximate country; never your raw IP address. Signed-in feature use is linked to your account only while detailed records exist.
  • Detailed records are deleted after about 30 days; only anonymous daily totals are kept longer. "Do Not Track" and Global Privacy Control are honored. A few counters keep their own anti-double-count records (directory clicks record an IP; profile visits keep an internal per-day record, shown only ever as totals).

Email

  • We send account emails (verification, password reset) and, occasionally, site news. Every bulk email carries an unsubscribe link for its category; staff may also email you individually about your account.
  • Delivery runs through an email provider acting on our behalf. Your address is never shown to other members.

Where your data lives

  • The Spire is hosted with Fly.io in the United States; using the site from elsewhere means your data is stored there. Connections are encrypted (HTTPS).
  • A limited number of rotating backups protect against data loss, so removed content can persist in a backup until newer ones replace it.

Outside services

  • Optional Google sign-in stores only your Google ID and email to link the sign-in; we get no further access to your Google account.
  • Videos (Theater and embeds) connect your browser to the provider, such as YouTube or Vimeo, under its own privacy policy. Fonts load from Google Fonts. Images added by URL load straight from their host, which can see your IP; chat images need a click before loading. Link-preview text is fetched by our server, though preview thumbnails load in your browser. Push notifications travel encrypted through your browser vendor and never include private message text.

Your data, your choices

  • "Export my data" in the Menu downloads your account details, profile, characters, journals, Worlds, stories, and uploaded images (chat and direct messages are not included).
  • You control profile visibility, direct-message availability, hiding 18+ content, notifications, and email categories. Blocking and /ignore work silently, with no notification to anyone.
  • To remove your account, contact staff. Removal deletes your account and content, keeping only the limited security, moderation, backup, and legal records described above. Content others quoted, exported, or built upon in shared works may survive removal.

When information leaves the site

  • Only when reasonably necessary to run the service, enforce rules, or protect users; when required by law or valid legal process; or to address a credible threat or child-safety concern, which may be reported to the appropriate authority. None of this gives staff access to private conversations.

This policy may be updated as features change; continued use means the updated policy applies. The Spire is for ages 13 and up.
